# Global Clinical & Evidence Reference Map

> 此文件只作 reference map。Explorer 不以此替代專業臨床判斷。

---

## 1. International / Global

- World Health Organization — Problem Management Plus (PM+): scalable psychological help for adults impaired by distress in communities exposed to adversity.
- World Health Organization — mhGAP: scaling up care for mental, neurological and substance use disorders.
- International Association for Suicide Prevention — crisis centres and helplines.
- Find a Helpline — global helpline lookup.

---

## 2. Depression

- NICE NG222 — Depression in adults: treatment and management.
- WHO scalable psychological interventions / PM+.

Useful non-medication concepts for Explorer:

- behavioral activation-informed planning
- meaningful activity tracking
- relapse prevention tracking
- shared decision and professional support boundary

---

## 3. Anxiety / Panic

- NICE CG113 — Generalised anxiety disorder and panic disorder in adults: management.
- WHO PM+ for problem management and stress-related distress.

Useful non-medication concepts:

- worry mapping
- uncertainty tolerance
- problem-solving
- grounding during acute panic

---

## 4. OCD

- NICE CG31 — Obsessive-compulsive disorder and body dysmorphic disorder: treatment.
- International OCD Foundation resources.

Useful non-medication concepts:

- CBT with exposure and response prevention as professional treatment model
- reassurance-seeking reduction
- compulsion tracking

---

## 5. Bipolar Mood Support

- NICE CG185 / updated evidence pages — Bipolar disorder: assessment and management.
- CANMAT / ISBD patient and family guide / clinician guideline resources.

Useful non-medication concepts:

- psychoeducation
- mood rhythm tracking
- sleep regularity
- relapse warning signs
- professional escalation for mania / mixed states

---

## 6. Social Anxiety

- NICE CG159 — Social anxiety disorder: recognition, assessment and treatment.

Useful non-medication concepts:

- self-focused attention tracking
- safety behavior mapping
- graded behavioral practice
- post-event rumination tracking

---

## 7. PTSD / Trauma

- NICE NG116 — Post-traumatic stress disorder.
- APA PTSD treatment guideline.
- VA National Center for PTSD.
- SAMHSA trauma-informed approaches.

Useful non-medication concepts:

- stabilization first
- grounding
- trauma-focused therapies only by trained clinicians
- no forced trauma retelling
- trigger awareness and safety planning

---
